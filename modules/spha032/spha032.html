SPHA032: Solid State Physics

2nd Semester, 3rd Level - University of Limpopo

Course Description

This course covers the fundamentals of solid state physics, including crystal structures, electronic properties, and various models of electrical and thermal behavior in solids. The module is designed to equip students with theoretical and practical knowledge applicable in fields such as material science, nanotechnology, and condensed matter physics.
